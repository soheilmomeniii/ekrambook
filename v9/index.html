Maker's Note

Ekram's best thinking on positioning was scattered across interviews, posts, and replies — sharp, but never in one place.

So I gathered it, organized it into a method you can actually use, then went back to Ekram to review and sharpen every part of it. What you're downloading isn't a recap of his ideas — it's those ideas turned into a working handbook, with steps, gates, and a single example carried all the way through.

I built it as a practitioner, for practitioners. If it helps you make something inevitable, that's the whole point.
